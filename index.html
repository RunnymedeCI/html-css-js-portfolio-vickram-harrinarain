    A Never-Ending Journey. In design, I want to achieve one simple goal: to continuously develop my creativity and sharpen my ability to bring my ideas to life. This ever-long goal of mine can’t be completed in a day, a month, or even a year but is something I can chase through a lifetime. Through this design journey so far I’ve made steps forward in reaching this goal in ways such as learning and sharpening skills in various digital design programs (Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, CapCut, ThinkerCAD, OBS, Blender, Figma, Webflow, etc.), understanding design principles and theories, and by just going through the process of experimenting with a key focus on trial and error and filtering. I feel I haven't achieved my life goal as of yet but I’m proud of my work so far.
